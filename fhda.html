About

We are a local dance studio specialising in RAD Ballet and NZAMD Jazz and have been running since 1996. We also offer contemporary, hip hop and lyrical classes as well as private tuition. The classes are taught by Fiona Haines, Chantelle Scoon and Emma Maddock and are located in Karori, Wellington. All of our classes are for students aged 3-18 years with an exception for hip hop which starts at age 7. We have a 100% exam pass rate, experienced and motivating teachers, fantastic end of year shows and fun, hardworking classes. We have also recently moved into a new studio.

Teachers

Fiona Haines - Principle, Ballet teacher

Classical – ARAD (Advanced Ballet), RDTS (Teachers exam - Distinction)
Jazz – ANZAMD (Advanced Jazz)
Training – NZ School of Dance (Wellington) and National Theatre Ballet School (Melbourne) FHDA has been running since 1996

Chantelle Scoon - Jazz

Chantelle began dancing at the age of 5, learning jazz, ballet, tap, lyrical and contemporary and she has successfully been awarded Solo Seal Diplomas for jazz and ballet in 2009. After receiving several performing arts scholarships, Chantelle attended full time contemporary and classical training at Unitec in Auckland. She trained under New Zealand Dance Company’s Sarah Foster-Sproull, Michael Parmenter and Kimbrian Bergh. Chantelle moved back to Wellington and has taught NZAMD jazz and competition work for various studios. In 2018 Chantelle took a group of FHDA students to LA to dance at Disneyland, and is planning on taking dancers to New York and Florida in 2020. This will be Chantelle’s 6th year with Fiona Haines Dance Academy

Emma Maddock - Junior Jazz and contemporary

Emma studied full time dance in Melbourne at Jason Coleman's Ministry of Dance specializing in contemporary and commercial dance. She has since worked at events throughout Australia and New Zealand, including Australia's Got Talent, the Logie Awards as well as music videos with Delta Goodrem and Kimbra. Emma currently choreographs for the pre-professional commercial dance students at Te Auaha in Wellington. This is Emma’s 5th year teaching at Fiona Haines Dance Academy
