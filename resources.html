Gear List

For your Adventurous Journey it is important to take the correct gear with you. Whether its summer or winter, you have to be prepared for anything.

  • Essential Equipment

    • Backpack 40-65 litres for overnight trips / 65-80 litres for multi-day trips
    • Waterproof pack liner
    • Sleeping bag
    • Head lamp and one set of spare batteries
    • Survival kit (make sure this is stored in a waterproof bag)
    • Survival bag or emergency blanket
    • Whistle (most Macpac packs have whistles built into the chest strap)
    • Pencil and paper
    • Fire steel and lighter
    • Knife (small and sharp is best)
    • Eating utensils: spork, bowl or plate, cup
    • Water bottle or bladder
    • Toiletries and medication
    • Sunscreen and insect repellent
    • Rubbish bag (all rubbish must be taken out with you)


  • Core Group Equipment

  • (carry at least one per group)
    • Mobile phone (emergency contact)
    • First-aid kit (include a blister kit, antihistamines and painkillers)
    • Map
    • Cooking equipment: Pot, Stove and gas
    • Cleaning equipment: Dishwashing liquid, cloth/scrubber, tea towel
    • Toilet paper
    • Hand sanitiser
    • Candles or battery operated lamp
    • Recommended: GPS (or app on phone)
    • Recommended: Personal Locator Beacon (PLB)
    • Recommended: Bivvy, fly or tarpaulin

  • Essential Clothing

    • Tramping Boots
    • Tramping socks x1 per day
    • Thermal base layer: top and leggings
    • Fast-drying shirt
    • Fast-drying shorts or pants
    • Mid-layer fleece
    • Warm hat and gloves
    • Sun hat and sunglasses
    • Rain jacket

  • Optional Kit

    • Sleeping bag liner
    • Tent
    • Sleeping mat (carry one incase hut is full
    • Camera
    • Neck buff
    • Trekking poles
    • Pack cover
    • Microfibre towel
    • Water treatment (purificaton tablets or system)
